ElderConnect Account Deletion

If you want your ElderConnect account and associated app data to be deleted, you can request deletion using one of the methods below.

In the app

If you are signed in as an Admin or Family Caller, you can use the in-app Delete my account option available from the account/settings screen.

If you are using ElderConnect as an Elder device, you can use the in-app account deletion option from the Elder standby screen settings.

By email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by email.

Contact email: michael.rybski@gmail.com

Suggested subject line: ElderConnect Account Deletion Request

Please include:

  • your account email address, if available
  • your username, if known
  • your role: Admin, Family Caller, or Elder
  • your family group name, if known

What will be deleted

When a deletion request is completed, ElderConnect will delete the relevant account and associated app data, including as applicable:

  • account credentials
  • username / profile information
  • family group membership references
  • device notification token(s)
  • temporary call/session data related to the deleted account

If an Admin deletes the managed family group, all associated Caller and Elder accounts in that group may also be permanently deleted.

What may be retained

Certain limited data may be retained only if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or regulatory obligations. Any retained data will be limited to what is necessary for those purposes.

Processing time

Deletion requests will be processed within a reasonable time after the request is verified.
